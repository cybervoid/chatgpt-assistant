Meditrack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Jan 11th, 2025

1. Type of Data Collected

- Email Address: We collect the user's email address through user input during the registration process.

2. Data Collection Method

- The email address is collected through user input upon registration.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

- The primary purpose of collecting the email address is to create and manage user accounts and enable secure authentication.

4. Data Storage and Security

- User data, including email addresses, is securely stored using industry-standard encryption methods. Data is managed within a protected environment designed to ensure security and privacy.

5. Third-Party Sharing

- We do not share any user information, including email addresses, with third parties.

6. Ads and Analytics

- Meditrack does not collect or use data for advertising or analytics purposes.

7. App Access and Permissions

- User authentication is facilitated using the email address, which is securely stored in a protected environment. This enables a seamless and secure user session experience.

8. Target Audience

- Meditrack is open for use by individuals of any age.

9. COVID-19 Contact Tracing

- Meditrack does not include any COVID-19 contact tracing or status functionality.

10. Data Deletion Requests

If you wish to have your data deleted, please send an email to our support team at meditrack@dumbhome.uk from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and ensure your data is permanently deleted.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at meditrack@dumbhome.uk.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the app or via email.
