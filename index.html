Meditrack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Dec 4th, 2023

1. Type of Data Collected:

Email Address: We collect the user's email address through user input during the registration process.

2. Data Collection Method:

The email address is collected through user input upon registration.

3. Purpose of Data Collection:

The primary purpose of collecting the email address is to create and manage user accounts.

4. Data Storage and Security:

User data, including email addresses, is stored securely behind a Virtual Private Cloud (VPC) to ensure the protection of sensitive information.

5. Third-Party Sharing:

We do not share any user information, including email addresses, with third parties.

6. Ads and Analytics:

Meditrack does not collect any data for advertising or analytics purposes.

7. App Access and Permissions:

User authentication is facilitated using the email address, which is securely stored in a database behind a VPC.

8. Target Audience:

Meditrack is open for use by individuals of any age.

9. COVID-19 Contact Tracing:

Meditrack does not include any COVID-19 contact tracing or status functionality.

Contact Information:

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at meditrack@dumbhome.uk.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the app or via email.

By using Meditrack, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
